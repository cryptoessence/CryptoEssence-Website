1- How To Use MyEtherWallet(MEW)


This is a guide to receive or send Crypto Essence Tokens using MEW (myetherwallet.com). Remember to test a small ammount for receive and send first. This will prevent your Tokens burn. Send a small amount of Ether to your address to have gas for sending tokens.

Generate Wallet

  1. Make sure you have a clean computer and cleared cookies and cache in your browser.
  2. Make sure you are in right site https://www.myetherwallet.com
  3. Enter a strong password (at least 9 characters).
  4. Write down your password and store it in safge place.
  5. Click Generate Wallet.


  1. Save you keystore file.
  2. Write or copy your Ethereum Address.
  3. Optional you can print your wallet and store in safe place.



Receive Tokens

To Receive tokens simply copy and paste your generated wallet address.  


Send Tokens

  1. Click Send Tokens tab.
  2. Select Keystore File.
  3. Select and open your wallet file.
  4. Write down your password and store it in safge place.
  5. Enter your password.
  6. Unlock your Wallet.

  1. Enter Receive Address.
  2. Enter Ammount to send.
  3. Click Token Balance to view your balance.
  4. If your token not listed, click Custom Token.
  5. Enter Contract Address, Symbol and Decimals.
  6. Click Save.
  7. Enter Gas ammount.
  8. Click Generate Transaction.

To look for your transaction use  ethplorer.io & etherscan.io


2- How to use MetaMask for Chrome

MetaMask is an Ethereum extension for your browser. It connects you to Ethereum applications (called dApps) easily and securely. MetaMask is also a digital wallet: you can store digital currencies in it and use it to buy…an album for instance!

To get started, all you need to do is install a Chrome extension. This three minute guide will show you how to do that.

Step 1:Click here to go to the Chrome Webstore and add the MetaMask plugin.


Step 2: Click “Add Extension” to confirm and MetaMask will be added.


Step 3: Read the privacy notice and click ‘Accept’.


Step 4: Scroll down the Terms of Use and click ‘Accept’ (the “Accept” button will only activate if you scroll the contract all the way down).


Step 5: Create your password. Make sure it’s secure!


Step 6: MetaMask will now give you a ‘seed phrase’ that we advise you to keep somewhere safe where you are sure to find it (and no one else).


Step 7: Congratulations! You now have your first Ethereum account :)
